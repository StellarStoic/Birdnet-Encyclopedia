Weather overlays

Compare detections with temperature, dew point, humidity, precipitation, pressure, wind, gusts, and weather conditions from Meteostat.

Your personal RapidAPI key lets the dashboard locate nearby Meteostat stations and request missing or selected hourly weather. Windows of two days or less use hourly values; longer windows use daily averages. Results are cached in this browser and refresh automatically after seven days, while "Update weather now" requests fresh data immediately. Each station lookup, metadata request, and hourly or daily data request can count against your RapidAPI plan. The application which owns the key must be subscribed to Meteostat's Basic plan.
