IUCN status
International Union for Conservation of Nature

The IUCN Red List measures a species' global risk of extinction.

LC Least ConcernEvaluated and currently at low risk of extinction. NT Near ThreatenedClose to qualifying for a threatened category. VU VulnerableFacing a high risk of extinction in the wild. EN EndangeredFacing a very high risk of extinction in the wild. CR Critically EndangeredFacing an extremely high risk of extinction in the wild. EW Extinct in the WildSurvives only in captivity or outside its former range. EX ExtinctNo reasonable doubt that the last individual has died. DD Data DeficientAvailable information is insufficient to assess extinction risk.
